Why ClearHealth

We’ve rethought the plan around three promises: transparency for members, dignity for providers, and ruthless simplicity for everyone.

Cash‑first simplicity

Pay the posted cash price or Medicare + 20%—whichever is lower. No networks to decode. No mystery bills.

Fast, fair payments

Providers get paid quickly on clean claims with minimal admin—and can still use their cash pricing.

Member‑first protection

Catastrophic coverage caps the worst‑case scenario while everyday care stays affordable and predictable.
