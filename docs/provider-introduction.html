Provider Introduction Packet (v1)

🩺 Welcome to ClearHealth

Simple. Transparent. Fast-Paying Health Coverage.

Hello! You’re receiving this because a patient in your office carries ClearHealth Basic, a new kind of health plan designed to make care simple for both patients and providers.

We’re not a traditional insurance company. We don’t use networks, prior authorizations, or complex claim forms. We just pay fairly — and fast.

💡 How ClearHealth Works

ClearHealth is a transparent, HSA-compatible health plan. Members pay directly for care until they meet their deductible, using their HSA or a linked card. Once their deductible is met, ClearHealth reimburses you at 120% of Medicare’s rate (or your published cash price, if lower).

No networks. No pre-approvals. No mystery pricing.

⚙️ How to Bill ClearHealth

You can use any of three easy methods — whichever fits your workflow.

Option 1: Quick Portal (Fastest)

  1. Go to clearhealth.life/providers
  2. Enter the patient’s Member ID and your CPT code(s).
  3. See the exact ClearHealth rate (120% of Medicare).
  4. Collect payment from the patient or submit the claim for payment.
  5. Receive ACH payment within 48 hours.

No login is required for simple lookups.

Option 2: Send an Invoice

If you already have a cash or self-pay process, simply:

  • Bill the patient directly at your usual cash price, or
  • Send the invoice to claims@clearhealth.life (PDF or itemized bill).

We’ll verify the service, process payment, and send ACH within two business days.
